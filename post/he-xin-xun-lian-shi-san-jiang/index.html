核心训练十三讲

2023-07-28

一直以来腹部的赘肉都很多，不过以前因为一直有跑步也喜欢做腹部的训练，所以几乎没什么焦虑。

但因为2021年春的lisfranc意外、保守决策和固有的多囊卵巢综合症，在无法跑步、未控制饮食的消极应对下，体重直线上升，直到21年11月份的出国体检出有脂肪肝（fatty liver），20年的国际旅行健康检查证明书（小红本）上写着体重是74.6kg，是从来没有料想到的体重。因为消极应对，所以直接选择忘记。出国后，受到身材、穿衣自由以及各类食物的吸引，我的逃避因子找到了理想的避难所。

转眼到了2023年，又面临出国，西班牙签证的体检比之前英国体检要繁复、详细得多，不仅给了我小红本，还给了我详细的检查报告，而首页就是检查结论，真像是论文的摘要呢。第一项是脂肪肝，第二项是丙氨酸氨基转移酶偏高，第三项是尿酸偏高。小红本上体重显示是77kg。这些数据无一不在告诉我不是一个健康的人，我必须要控制饮食以及减重。想起以前在我体重60kg的时候，半仙戏谑问什么时候到75kg。没想到居然有实现的一天。真的，什么都有可能发生啊。

2023年7月的最后几天，看到了比较有意思的腹部训练，有不少没见过的动作，所以想记录一下。然后就有了上面的一段文字，可以说是真正让我直面了77kg体重的事实，从2021年11月-2023年7月，整整20个月期间，我从来没有称重过，这不是逃避又是什么。或许等我体重下来，以上三个健康问题都木有了，我就会整个视频版本。

2023.10.14在格村的第二个礼拜六，新到了体重秤，是 75.45kg。
2023.11.1，我的体重是74上下浮动，取决于穿多少衣服和喝多少水。我希望在12月1日那天我的体重能到70kg。虽然也是我曾经没有想过的体重，但是就目前来看仍然是一个不小的挑战。

Straight Crunch

  • Fingertips are by the ears
  • Press ab back into the floor
  • Reach for opposite toes
    [1]

Leg Drops

  • straight legs or bent legs
  • straight legs, do not touch the floor, down and up
  • bent legs, tapping the heel to the floor and bringing them up

Plank Knees INS

  • bring one leg into the chest at a time, keep the body nice and straight,no booties in the air
  • bring knees as close as you can to the chest

Lay Down Star Crunchs

  • arms are straight, legs are bent. bring them up for a crunch at the same time
  • bring ab really back into the floor and bring bring everything up together, or we can say squeeze ab. contract everything and suck it in through the center

Single Leg Pulses

  • start off with elbows, really try to tuck the booty,sitting almost like on the top of the bum
  • using lower abs, extending one leg at a time, pulsing bottom leg
  • keep chest lifted up, do not sink down into the shoulders
    [2]

Elbow to Knee Crunches

  • on you backs, legs are up
  • bring the elbow to the knee, lifting shoulder blade up off the ground when crunching

Reverse Crunches

  • extend the legs at the bottom or keep the legs bent
  • bring it in really focus on the crunch,focus on the lower abs, and think about driving those feet up to the ceiling.

Plank Pikes

  • on your elbow, drive one knee in as you press the upper body backwards
  • coming down into the straight line every time, do not keep that butt poked up in the air
  • really think about the lower abs as the top of your triangle

Side Crunches

  • in and outs crunches, really think about contracting the sides into the ground

Hold Plank

  • hold in a nice straight line or you can add some hip dips, or you can rock forward and back
  • you can do some commando planks

Ab Pulse or Twist

  • sittting on your bum, you can either pulse it out, sitting back making sure you are keeping the tension on the whole time
  • or you can twist it out, do several twists.
  • just keep the tension on the whole time

Cobra to Childs

  • come into a cobra position,letting hips feel nice and heavy
  • push through from here all the way back into child's pose, sending hips back
  • roll throught the spine, back into the cobra pose

Cat and Cow

  • let belly feel nice and heavy arch that back and send the top of your head all the way back
  • and then from here to the exact position so belly button to the ceiling.

Side Stretches

  • come down onto your knees
  • reach one arm up, coming all the way over, taking some nice breaths in this position and switch

ss

  • come down onto your back
  • arms are in a goal post position, the back of your palms are on the floor
  • drop the knees down to the one side and your head face the opposite direction
  • move through center to switch
